how to play

The aim of scrambled is to solve as many anagrams as you can.

Before clicking new game, select the length of the anagrams: 5, 6 or 7 letters.

You have three lives. You lose a life if you enter an incorrect answer so type carefully!

You also lose a life if you choose to give up on an anagram and press reveal.

You can shuffle the letters of the anagram as many times as you like.

The game is over when you run out of lives or if you click end game.
